# Code of Conduct

votechain-machine is a project for trusted election infrastructure research and development.
All participants are expected to maintain respectful, constructive, and professional collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and clear in all discussions.
- Focus feedback on technical work and protocol outcomes.
- Be patient with contributors and newcomers.
- Keep confidential or sensitive details out of public threads.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, doxxing, or targeted abuse.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Repeated spam or bad-faith disruption.
- Intimidation in technical discussion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, block, or ban participants who violate these expectations.
Appeals can be requested through the contact in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).

## Scope

This applies to:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Public discussions tied to this repository
- Any direct coordination around project artifacts and releases
